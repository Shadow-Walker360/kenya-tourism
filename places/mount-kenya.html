Welcome to Mount Kenya

Rising to 5,199 meters (17,057 ft), Mount Kenya is Africa’s second-highest mountain and the pride of the nation. Revered by the Kikuyu people as the home of God (*Ngai*), its snow-capped peaks pierce the clouds, feeding rivers and nurturing life across Kenya. It’s not just a mountain — it’s a living symbol of strength, heritage, and timeless beauty.

The slopes of Mount Kenya are home to dense forests, glittering lakes, bamboo thickets, and alpine meadows where elephants roam under a cathedral of stars. Whether you’re here to climb, hike, or simply breathe the sacred air, Mount Kenya is a journey of the soul.

Things to Do Around Mount Kenya

  • Climb to Point Lenana: The most accessible summit for trekkers — sunrise views here are divine.
  • Explore Mount Kenya National Park: Home to elephants, buffaloes, leopards, and colobus monkeys.
  • Visit Lake Ellis and Lake Michaelson: Two breathtaking alpine lakes fed by glacial meltwater.
  • Camping Under the Stars: Spend the night beneath Africa’s Milky Way — an experience you’ll never forget.
  • Cultural Tours: Meet the Kikuyu and Meru communities who share ancient legends about the mountain.

Fun Facts About Mount Kenya

  • 🏔️ Mount Kenya is an **extinct volcano** that erupted over 3 million years ago.
  • ❄️ Despite being near the equator, its peaks remain covered with **glaciers** year-round (though shrinking due to climate change).
  • 🐘 The mountain’s forests are home to the rare **bongo antelope**, one of Kenya’s most elusive animals.
  • 🌍 The mountain has **three main peaks**: Batian (5199m), Nelion (5188m), and Lenana (4985m).
  • ☀️ The Kikuyu believe **God (Ngai)** lives at the summit — they pray facing the mountain.
  • 🎬 Mount Kenya appeared in the movie *The Snows of Kilimanjaro* as a symbol of adventure and beauty.
  • 💧 Its glaciers feed the **Tana River**, Kenya’s longest and most vital waterway.

Hidden Gems of Mount Kenya

💎 *Chogoria Route* — The most scenic ascent, passing waterfalls and lakes so blue they look unreal. 💎 *Castle Forest Lodge* — A hidden retreat surrounded by bamboo groves and elephant trails. 💎 *Naro Moru Caves* — Used by Mau Mau freedom fighters during Kenya’s struggle for independence.
